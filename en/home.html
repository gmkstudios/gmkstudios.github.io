How we work

We are a very slow but organized team, we do things calmly to prevent serious errors.
TDoaND will be created with a very famous software, one of the best:
Unreal Engine 4, which over time could become Unreal Engine 5.
We don't have the best performance at the moment, in fact we are trying to raise funds to give to players

How much will the video game cost?

TDoaND will be a 100% free game, in offline / story mode, subsequently, after having
the necessary funds, we may also decide to implement the mode in the game Online.
TDoaND will be free for first players only, after which the price will be set at around € 10 / € 15.
In the course of the time, you can enter the Official Server of GMK Studios and support us in the creation,
provide some advice etc... You have also the possibility of donating a few €, this action will be of gigantic help
and will speed us up a lot in creating the video game. We accept wire transfers via PayPal and PaySafeCard.
